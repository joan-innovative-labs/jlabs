Our Product

Namma Garage

Namma Garage is an in-house product launched and owned by Joan Innovative Labs, built to simplify how people discover, buy, and sell vehicles in India.

Designed specifically for the automotive ecosystem, the platform connects buyers with verified listings while helping sellers reach the right audience through a transparent, user-friendly experience.

This product reflects our product-first mindset — combining strong engineering, clean UX, and deep understanding of local mobility needs.
